## 3. Restaurant & Chef Partnership Model

### 3.1 Restaurant Partner Tiers

| Tier | Monthly Fee | Commission per Order | Features |
|------|-------------|---------------------|----------|
| **Starter** | €49/month | 15% | Basic listing, order management |
| **Growth** | €149/month | 12% | Featured placement, analytics, promo tools |
| **Premium** | €399/month | 10% | Top placement, AI menu suggestions, POS integration |
| **Flagship** | €999/month | 8% | Exclusive features, white-glove service, global reach |

### 3.2 Restaurant Value Proposition

```
Cerex offers restaurants:
├── Access to 50M+ food enthusiasts globally
├── AI-powered demand forecasting (reduce waste by 20%)
├── Automated translation to 50+ languages
├── Cultural context pages that drive tourist/expat orders
├── Analytics: best-performing dishes, peak hours, customer demographics
├── Ingredient sourcing connections (reduce costs by 10-15%)
└── Marketing tools: sponsored content, promotional campaigns
```

### 3.3 Chef Monetization

Professional chefs on the platform can monetize through:

1. **Recipe Monetization**: Set recipes as "Premium" (€0.99 - €4.99 per access)
   - Cerex takes 30% commission (similar to App Store model)
   - Chef receives 70% per sale

2. **Live Cooking Classes**: Integrated video streaming
   - Cerex takes 20% commission
   - Average session: €29.99 for 60-minute live class

3. **Consulting Revenue**: Restaurants hire chefs via platform
   - Cerex takes 10% platform fee
   - Average consulting: €150/hour

4. **Branded Content**: Cerex pays top chefs for exclusive content
   - Signing bonus: €5,000 - €50,000 for exclusive recipes
   - Monthly content stipend: €500 - €5,000

### 3.4 Restaurant Fraud Prevention Impact

By detecting and preventing fraudulent restaurants, Cerex:
- Maintains platform trust (NPS impact: +15 points)
- Reduces chargebacks (saving estimated €200K/year at scale)
- Improves legitimate restaurant success rates

## 4. Advertising Model

### 4.1 Ad Formats

| Format | Placement | CPM (Cost per 1000) | CPC (Cost per Click) |
|--------|-----------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Native Recipe Card | Search results, feed | €8 - €15 | €0.35 - €0.80 |
| Sponsored Chef Profile | Explorer pages | €12 - €20 | €0.50 - €1.20 |
| Ingredient Banner | Recipe detail page | €5 - €10 | €0.20 - €0.50 |
| Video Pre-roll | Cooking videos | €15 - €25 | N/A |
| Full-Screen Story | Mobile app onboarding | €20 - €35 | €0.80 - €2.00 |

### 4.2 Advertising Targeting Capabilities

```
Targeting Dimensions:
├── Geographic: Country, City, Region
├── Cuisine Preferences: "Users who cook Asian food 3x/week"
├── Dietary: "Vegan users looking for plant-based recipes"
├── Intent: "Users actively searching for birthday cake recipes"
├── Behavioral: "Users who ordered from Italian restaurants last month"
├── Lookalike: "Users similar to your best customers"
└── Cultural Affinity: "West African diaspora in Europe"
```

### 4.3 Advertising Standards

- **Frequency Cap**: Max 1 sponsored item per 5 organic results
- **Labeling**: All ads clearly labeled "Sponsored" or "Promoted"
- **Quality Score**: Minimum relevance score required (prevents irrelevant ads)
- **Free Users Only**: Zero ads for Explorer, Chef Pro, Enterprise subscribers
- **No Data Selling**: Cerex never sells user data to advertisers

## 5. Commission on Orders

### 5.1 Commission Structure

```
Order of €25.00
├── Restaurant receives:         €20.75 (83%)   → After 17% commission
├── Cerex commission:             €4.25 (17%)
│   ├── Payment processing (Stripe): -€1.00    → ~4% of order value
│   ├── Platform operating cost:     -€1.50    → Infrastructure, support
│   └── Net Cerex margin:            €1.75     → 7% net margin per order
└── Delivery fee:                 €2.90         → Separate from order value
    ├── Driver payment:              €2.20
    └── Cerex logistics margin:      €0.70
```

### 5.2 Commission Negotiation

- New restaurants (first 3 months): 10% flat (introductory rate)
- Volume bonus: >500 orders/month → -2% commission discount
- Exclusive partnership: 3-year exclusive → down to 8%
- Premium tier restaurants get pre-negotiated rates

### 5.3 Ingredient Kit Model

Alternative to restaurant orders: Cerex sells ingredient kits for home cooking.

```
Ingredient Kit Revenue Model:
├── Recipe for 4 persons, kit price: €34.99
├── Ingredient sourcing cost:        €18.00  (51%)

├── Gross profit:                    €8.99   (26% margin)
└── Marketing/overhead allocation:   €3.00   (9%)
    Net contribution:                €5.99   (17% net)
```

## 8. Growth Strategy

### 8.1 Go-to-Market by Phase

**Phase 1: Community Building (0-6 months)**
- Target food bloggers and Instagram chefs (10K-500K followers)
- Provide free Chef Pro accounts to 1,000 influencers
- Focus markets: France, UK, USA (English + French bilingual)
- TikTok cooking content strategy: 3 videos/day

**Phase 2: Marketplace Growth (6-18 months)**
- Partner with 500 premium restaurants in 5 cities: Paris, London, New York, Lagos, Dubai
- Launch "Cerex Culinary Week" events (pop-ups + online)
- Podcast: "Cerex World Kitchen" (weekly, 30-minute episodes)
- Press strategy: target food publications in 10 countries

**Phase 3: Global Expansion (18-36 months)**
- Localized apps in 20 languages
- Regional content teams in: Africa, Southeast Asia, South America
- Partnership with major food festivals globally
- B2B: corporate wellness meal planning, hospital nutrition

### 8.2 Viral Growth Mechanisms

1. **Recipe Collaboration**: Two chefs collaborate on a recipe → both audiences notified
2. **Cooking Challenges**: Weekly global challenges (#CerexChallenge) with prizes
3. **Cultural Passport**: Gamification — earn stamps for cooking recipes from different countries
4. **Friend Referral**: €5 credit for both parties when a friend subscribes
5. **Restaurant Gift Cards**: Users can send restaurant credit as gifts
6. **AI-generated "Your Culinary Year" report**: Shareable Spotify Wrapped-style recap
